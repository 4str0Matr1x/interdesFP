Description

"Modify Your Mp3" is a data visualization that analyzes the bits in the mp3 file and creates rainbow-colored soundwaves on the screen. So it can work with both music or other types of sound. This was made by me with a reference from "Audio Viz - Starter" by Mike Brondbjerg. I made this to be very interactive through p5js in way that's like a mini-Audacity.
